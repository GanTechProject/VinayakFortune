#### Executive Summary

Solo accountants in the US spend an average of 11 hours per week on regulatory compliance (state and federal), with quarterly peaks of 20+ hours. This represents a $9.4B addressable market (US solo + 2-person firms), growing at 6.2% CAGR. Demand signals are strong (rising search volume, recurring forum complaints) and competitor offerings are fragmented, with no dominant AI-native product. The opportunity scores **7.6 / 10** on the default rubric, with **high confidence**.

**Recommendation:** Pursue as a v1.0 SaaS product. Target solo accountants and 2-person firms in the US; expand to UK / CA / AU in v2.

**Top 3 risks:**

1. Regulatory regime changes can invalidate the core value prop overnight.
2. Established tax software (Intuit, Wolters Kluwer) can ship a similar AI co-pilot in 12–18 months.
3. The 1099 vs. W-2 distinction creates a sub-segmentation that doubles the surface area.

**Top 3 opportunities:**

1. Strong pull from public forums (Reddit r/Accounting, TaxProTalk) for AI help.
2. Existing tools are workflow-oriented, not AI-native; a 10x UX is feasible.
3. White-label and accounting-firm partnership channels are underused.
